The ‘Azimut 76’ is a practical large luxury motor yacht, built by the very well respected Italian boat builder to an exceptional standard of luxury as expected with the ‘Azimut’ brand.
Although the boat was built in 1987 she has recently in 2013 been extensively refitted both inside and out.
She is fitted with a comprehensive set of top grade equipment making her a suitable candidate for coastal or extended offshore cruising.
The purpose built 76ft fast cruising yacht, has an extended rear swim and docking platform bringing the yacht to 80 feet in length in total.
Below decks the accommodation is roomy and well laid out. The main deck comprises of a saloon entered directly from the cockpit area.
The lower deck level is accessed by stairs at the fore and aft ends of the saloon leading to two cabins forward, the master berth in the forward “V” and a two berth cabin on the starboard side.
At the after end there is a further luxurious double berth. At the fore end of the saloon there is a well equipped galley imported from KIVK kitchens in Denmark.
Access to the boat is from the after swim platform into the cockpit and a moulded stairway leads to the flybridge.
The Swim Platform is equipped with easy access ladders ,
fishing pole holders and a large Stainless BBQ along with underwater LED lighting which makes for a pleasant evening swim.
The flybridge is fitted with a small bar, a small dining table and at the aft end a sun deck with tan vinyl half round settee
and chairs,there is storage right aft for a 10 man liferaft and and 2 x jet ski’s which can be dropped into the water via a 1000 kg crane type davit.
A further jet ski is also carried on the rear of the yacht.
Usage of the Jet ski’s are complimentary when chartering this luxurious yacht along with an extensive list of water toys.
Entertainment is also provided by a full DVD sound system and a Samsung 55” flat screen TV in the splendid saloon.
