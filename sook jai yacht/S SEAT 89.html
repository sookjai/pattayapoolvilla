This incredible stylish yacht is available for up to 16 VIP passengers on a day charter while sleeping 6 in the 3 superbly
decorated cabins that come complete with their own bathrooms and the latest in home entertainment.

relax while enjoying the amazing home theater system on offer.

Up on the fly bridge you'll find it a great place to sit back and enjoy the stunning views your journey will present you

If you wish to cruise the beautiful waters of the Pattaya Archipelago or further afield in complete style
