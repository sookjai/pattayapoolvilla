This fantastic multi-level catamaran is an extremely practical vessel. She is a ‘one of a kind’ in Pattaya and the features are quite something. This magnificent catamaran has two internal full-size decks as well as a flybridge, and large Bow and aft decks. The internal decks are fully air-conditioned and fitted out to a very high standard which is to be expected from a ‘Schionning’ designed catamaran. No expense has been spared on the fixtures and fittings on this beautiful vessel, there is plenty of spacious seating and a bar on each deck to serve your guests while they relax in comfort and take in the picturesque views of the Pattaya archipelago. The ‘Schionning 62’ comes equipped with rather a large selection of Watersports activities, such as; Kayaking, Stand-up Paddle Boarding, Snorkeling, Fishing and even Scuba Diving is available from the catamaran, the ‘Schionning 62’ really is the complete package. The kitchen is fitted with high-end equipment and there is a very attractive breakfast bar arrangement on one of the internal decks and also a fantastic cocktail bar. There is really no shortage of features on this amazing vessel. On the flybridge of this stunning catamaran, there is ample space to party, relax in comfort and enjoy the views. The aft deck is also very generously fitted out with large sun loungers which are perfectly contoured for maximum relaxation on the ocean and a there is also superb al fresco dining arrangement where you can wine and dine in style whilst taking in the beautiful island views. The aft deck also features a plunge pool, giving you the perfect surroundings to toast your champagne. At the bow of the ‘Schionning 62’ there is also a very large c section table and seating combination giving you amazing 360-degree viewing of the Pattaya archipelago. Even the 2 interior decks boast very large windows for maximum comfort and visibility. Wherever you are on this amazing luxury catamaran, you can be sure that the viewing of Pattaya’s stunning scenery is at it’s very best.
