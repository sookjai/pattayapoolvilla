We are proud to present the ‘Regal 3260 Commodore’.
This fantastic vessel comes across as a seriously attractive motor cruiser with it’s sleek lines, a good turn of speed,
fully air-conditioned interior, comfortable accommodation, endless gadgets as well as more room than expected for a 35 footer.
With the performance of a fast speedboat, the ‘Regal 3260 Commodore’ is the ideal way for family and friends to enjoy the sights
and sounds of the Pattaya Archipelago and further afield while travelling in style.
The spacious helm features a triple wide electric bench seat and more comfortable seating for all towards the aft.
Behind the helm is comfortable seating for seven and a table for al fresco dining which can be lowered and covered to provide an exterior king sized bed.
Further back at the stern there is an oversized extended swim platform from where you can easily disembark to enjoy
visiting a deserted beach, swimming or snorkeling in the beautiful clear waters through which you are travelling.
When returning to the boat there is a hot and cold transom shower so you can rinse off before re-entering the vessel and enjoying the rest of your day.
Forward towards the bow there is a large soft sun pad which is the ideal place to completely relax or to get some serious sunbathing done. Throughout the interior and exterior of the boat there is comfortable carpet flooring.
The interior is well designed and includes a fully functional galley, a good sized forward v-berth which sleeps two and a roomy dinette sofa.
The aft cabin features a king-sized bed large enough to offer quiet and privacy for family or guests on those overnight trips.
The bathroom has all the comforts of home including an electric toilet, hot and cold water shower and sink and good cabinet space.
