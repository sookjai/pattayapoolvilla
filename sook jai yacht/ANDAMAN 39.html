The ‘Andaman 39’ is a high performance fast sailing catamaran that offers an exhilarating experience
that will bring a smile to sailing enthusiasts and novices alike. At just under 12 metres in length and 6 metres wide
there is a light and airy feel to the vessel, this is accompanied with ample deck space and a spacious cockpit
allowing passengers to travel in comfort whilst sitting back and relaxing.
Being a lightweight catamaran the boat has an easy motion that is very comfortable for passengers to enjoy a stable ride.
Another benefit of this delightful catamaran is that the sailing controls are conveniently located at a comfortable
height so making the handling of the boat relatively easy.
This set up means that the ‘Andaman 39’ lends itself to being an easy to handle vessel for competent sailors
and a great boat for corporate team building or for those that want to be hands on and learn more about sailing.
Inside this fantastic vessel there is the lounge area complete with comfortable sofa seating and table.
There is no need for air conditioning as the open plan style allows for plenty of cool sea breeze to flow through and 360 degree views.
For the overnight journey you will find two double cabins midships and two single cabins forward.
The galley is located on the bridgedeck with a two burner stove plus a toaster oven.
At the stern of each hull you will find a spacious head with an electric marine toilet and shower.
The ‘Andaman 39’ also comes equipped with a 3 meter Yachtmaster RIB with a 3 hp Yamaha outboard motor to enable easy beach access.
To explore the beautiful deserted island coves there is also a two seater ocean kayak carried on board for you to use
as well quality snorkeling and fishing equipment
